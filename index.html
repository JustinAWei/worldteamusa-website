Our Training Programs

We strive for superiority through our unique curriculum which combines a modern approach and traditional values. Through the years, our unique programs have brought about dramatic and positive changes in so many people.

Muay Thai

The Art of Eight Limbs. Learn authentic Thai boxing techniques including punches, kicks, knees, and elbows through pad work, bag training, and conditioning. Beginners start with stance, footwork, and basic strikes while developing traditional techniques, cardio conditioning, self-defense skills, and mental discipline. All fitness levels welcome.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

The gentle art of ground fighting. Master leverage, technique, and strategy over strength. Learn positions, escapes, submissions, and transitions with focus on technique and timing. Develops ground grappling skills and problem-solving abilities. Great for smaller people who want to use technique over strength.

Mixed Martial Arts

Combine striking and grappling. The complete martial arts experience for serious athletes featuring integration of all martial arts, sparring, technique drilling, and fight preparation. Covers stand-up striking, ground fighting, takedowns & wrestling, and competition preparation. For dedicated athletes only.

Kids & Family Classes

Fun, safe martial arts training for children and families. Build confidence and discipline together through games, basic techniques, respect, and discipline in a fun, supportive environment. Features age-appropriate training, character development, family bonding, and anti-bullying skills.

6-Week Bootcamp

High-intensity fitness program using martial arts-inspired movements. Perfect for those who want the workout without the fighting. Features full-body workouts, cardio conditioning, and strength training in a supportive group environment. 45-minute classes, 3 times per week with kickboxing-inspired moves focused on weight loss and stress relief.

Private Lessons

Personalized one-on-one training tailored to your specific goals, whether beginner self-defense or advanced competition preparation. Includes focused instruction at your pace, technique refinement, and personalized feedback from expert coaches. Features customized training plan, flexible scheduling, and faster skill development. All skill levels welcome.

Meet Our Expert Instructors

We believe that in order to service our students properly, all instructors must be totally committed to the martial arts. Our staff goes beyond the call of duty to teach students the self-respect, confidence, and discipline that we have learned through the practice of martial arts.

Kru Sam Phimsoutham

Owner & Head Instructor

Authentic Thai/Lao trainer with generational Muay Thai heritage. Certified under Master Toddy, Kru Sam has trained 52 champions including 18 world champions over 30+ years.

Kru Sam learned Muay Thai from his father, specifically Muay Boran and Lerdrit, which run in his family for generations. His father was a trainer and referee in Lumpini Stadium, and his uncle as well. His first lesson in Muay Boran was given to him when he was four years old.

He immigrated to the United States at age seven. At the age of 18 Kru Sam then trained under Master Toddy for 10 years to become a certified Muay Thai trainer and teacher. He brought Muay Thai to the Bay Area in 1991.

Kru Sam has developed ordinary people with zero experience into World Class Fighters, leading generations to go from zero to Champion, both in the ring and outside. All champions give back to the school and community.

Achievements: 52 Champions Trained (18 World Champions), Certified by Master Toddy (Bangkok, Thailand), 30+ Years Teaching Experience, Introduced Muay Thai to Bay Area (1991), Muay Boran & Lerdrit Family Lineage

Classes: Competition Muay Thai, Elite Muay Thai, Power Fitness & Cardio Conditioning

Our Story

A Legacy Built on Authentic Martial Arts Heritage

World Team USA will teach you about your strengths, how to build your confidence, how to relieve your stress and how to find the discipline to reach your goals. Live your dream!

Since bringing Muay Thai to the Bay Area in 1991, we've developed four generations of champions - homegrown fighters raised with intensity, passion, and dedication. Our staff goes beyond the call of duty to teach students the self-respect, confidence, and discipline learned through authentic martial arts practice.

We train smart and we train fair. No attitude, no hierarchy ‚Äî everyone starts on an even playing field. Through dedication and perseverance, all goals in life are within reach.

We strive for superiority through our unique curriculum which combines a modern approach with traditional values. Students learn to set short, intermediate, and long-term goals - each level important for achieving success, not just in martial arts, but in everyday life.

We wholeheartedly believe there is no superior style of martial arts and no such thing as having the best school. We offer training only to those whose personality, goals and lifestyle align with our philosophy of total commitment to the martial arts.

Led by Kru Sam Phimsoutham

Owner & Master Instructor

Thai/Lao trainer with generational Muay Thai heritage - Muay Boran and Lerdrit run in his family for generations. His father was a Thai General, trainer and referee in Lumpini Stadium. Kru Sam learned his first lesson at age four, immigrated at seven, and at 18 trained for 10 years under Master Toddy, whose father knew his father. He pioneered Muay Thai in the Bay Area by fighting black belts wherever he could until word spread to legends like Sifu Harlan Lee and the Lozada family.

Through the years, our unique programs have brought about dramatic and positive changes in so many people that we're always eager for the opportunity to share them with others. Joining us will be one of the most rewarding activities for you or your family. All our champions give back to the school and community, supporting the next generation of martial artists.
