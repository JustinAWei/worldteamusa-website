Our Training Programs

We strive for superiority through our unique curriculum which combines a modern approach and traditional values. Through the years, our unique programs have brought about dramatic and positive changes in so many people.

Muay Thai

The Art of Eight Limbs. Learn authentic Thai boxing techniques including punches, kicks, knees, and elbows through pad work, bag training, and conditioning. Develop traditional techniques, cardio conditioning, self-defense skills, and mental discipline through stance, footwork, and striking combinations.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

The gentle art of ground fighting. Master leverage, technique, and strategy over strength. Learn positions, escapes, submissions, and transitions with emphasis on technique rather than strength and power. Develops ground grappling skills and problem-solving abilities.

Mixed Martial Arts

The fastest growing sport in the world. Combine striking and grappling in this complete martial arts experience featuring integration of all disciplines. Learn stand-up striking, ground fighting, takedowns & wrestling through technique drilling and controlled practice. Our curriculum teaches the same skills to all students, regardless of whether you plan to compete.

Kids & Family Classes

Fun, safe martial arts training for children and families. Build confidence and discipline together through games, basic techniques, respect, and discipline in a supportive environment. Features age-appropriate training, character development, family bonding, and anti-bullying skills.

6-Week Bootcamp

High-intensity fitness program using martial arts-inspired movements. Perfect for those who want the workout without the fighting. Features full-body workouts, cardio conditioning, and strength training in a supportive group environment. 45-minute classes, 3 times per week with kickboxing-inspired moves focused on weight loss and stress relief.

Private Lessons

Personalized one-on-one training tailored to your specific goals, whether beginner self-defense or advanced competition preparation. Includes focused instruction at your pace, technique refinement, and personalized feedback from expert coaches. Features customized training plan, flexible scheduling, and faster skill development.

Our Story

A Legacy Built on Authentic Martial Arts Heritage

World Team USA will teach you about your strengths, how to build your confidence, how to relieve your stress and how to find the discipline to reach your goals. Live your dream!

Since bringing Muay Thai to the Bay Area in 1991, we've developed four generations of champions - homegrown fighters raised with intensity, passion, and dedication. Our staff goes beyond the call of duty to teach students the self-respect, confidence, and discipline learned through authentic martial arts practice.

We train smart and we train fair. No attitude, no hierarchy — everyone starts on an even playing field. Through dedication and perseverance, all goals in life are within reach.

We strive for superiority through our unique curriculum which combines a modern approach with traditional values. Students learn to set short, intermediate, and long-term goals - each level important for achieving success, not just in martial arts, but in everyday life.

We believe that in order to service our students properly, all instructors must be totally committed to the martial arts. We respect every student's different goals, whether it's to become a world champion or simply to nurture the mind, body and spirit.

Founded by Kru Sam Phimsoutham

Owner & Master Instructor

Thai/Lao trainer with generational Muay Thai heritage - Muay Boran and Lerdrit run in his family for generations. His father was a Thai General, trainer and referee in Lumpini Stadium, and his uncle as well. Kru Sam received his first lesson in Muay Boran at age four.

After immigrating to the United States at age seven, Kru Sam trained for 10 years under Master Toddy (whose father knew his father) to become certified as "Kru." Because knowing how to fight doesn't always translate to knowing how to teach others, Kru Sam's innate teaching ability, combined with his Muay Boran and Lerdrit background and Master Toddy certification, are keys to his success.

Kru Sam brought Muay Thai to the Bay Area in 1991, introducing the sport by fighting black belts wherever he could. Word quickly spread to legends like Sifu Harlan Lee, Sifu Shane Lacey, and the Lozada family. SFPD Officer Paul Lozada gave Kru Sam his first teaching job at a small academy in downtown San Francisco, where he taught part-time for years, raising fighters including notable names like Alex Gong.

When Kru Sam opened Team USA nearly 30 years ago, he raised his second generation of champions. Today, he's on his fourth generation of champions, and the team continues to grow. Along with Coach Cung Le and Professor Navarros, Kru Sam brought the first "smoker" to the Bay Area in 1995, helping establish the region's martial arts competition scene.

Through the years, our unique programs have brought about dramatic and positive changes in so many people that we're always eager for the opportunity to share them with others. Joining us will be one of the most rewarding activities for you or your family. All our champions give back to the school and community, supporting the next generation of martial artists.
